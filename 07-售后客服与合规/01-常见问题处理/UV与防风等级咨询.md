# UV与防风等级咨询

## 适用场景

买家询问红色、米色或其他颜色伞布的 UV rating / UPF rating，以及产品是否有 wind rating / wind speed rating。

## 已确认边界

- 当前不得自行提供具体 UPF、UV 等级、防风等级或风速数值。
- 可以说明伞布用于提供遮阳并帮助减少阳光直射。
- 可以提醒悬臂伞伞面较大，起风、暴雨或恶劣天气应收拢并固定。
- 可以提醒底座需要按说明正确加重。
- `12-15 mph` 可作为客服预期管理中的谨慎收伞建议，但必须说明这是安全使用建议，不得表述为产品 `wind rating`、认证或测试结果。

## 建议英文回复

```text
Dear Customer,

Thank you for your interest in JZAC & QZYD.

Both the red and beige canopy fabrics are designed to provide shade and help reduce direct sun exposure for outdoor use. However, we do not currently have a specific published UPF or UV rating number for these fabric colors, so we do not want to provide an unverified rating.

Regarding wind, this cantilever umbrella does not have a specific wind-speed rating. Because cantilever umbrellas have a large canopy surface, we recommend closing and securing the umbrella during windy conditions, heavy rain, or severe weather. For stable use, please also make sure the base is properly weighted according to the product setup instructions.

Please let us know if you have any other questions. We will be happy to help.

Best regards,
Customer Support Team
JZAC & QZYD
```

## 待确认

- 是否有供应链或测试报告支持具体 UPF/UV 等级。
- 是否有任何官方风速测试、认证或说明书评级。
- 不同颜色伞布是否存在 UV 表现差异。
- 是否将 `12-15 mph` 写入说明书、A+ 或客服标准话术；正式采用前需补充 Review 截图、说明书或供应商确认。
