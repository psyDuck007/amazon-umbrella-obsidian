# 产地与美国履约咨询

## 适用场景

买家询问品牌、公司、制造地、发货地、美国仓、退货是否需要寄回海外等问题。

## 合规边界

- 可以直接说明产品制造地，但不要夸大设计、工程、制造全流程，除非有证据。
- 不要写 `all inventory is fully stocked`、`never`、`always` 等绝对承诺。
- 不要具体写 Georgia、Illinois、New Jersey 等仓库州名，除非该订单或当前库存已确认。
- 不要承诺平台规则外的退货、补发、运费承担或本地售后条件。
- 所有沟通应继续通过 Amazon messages，不引导站外联系。

## 建议英文回复

```text
Dear Customer,

Thank you for your interest in JZAC & QZYD.

To answer your question directly, our patio umbrellas are manufactured in China.

For customers in the United States, orders are typically fulfilled from US-based warehouses when inventory is available, so shipping is handled domestically through the Amazon order process.

If a return or replacement is needed, we will assist you through the applicable Amazon return or support process. You do not need to arrange an international return on your own unless Amazon specifically instructs otherwise.

If you have any other questions about the product, please feel free to contact us through Amazon messages.

Best regards,
Customer Support Team
JZAC & QZYD
```

## 待确认

- 是否可以公开具体美国仓州名。
- 当前订单实际发货仓库和退货仓库。
- 品牌公司主体、制造主体和设计主体的标准对外表述。
